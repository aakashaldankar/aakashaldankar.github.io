Adam Young could sing a newspaper paragraph.

I would still listen.

And I would probably enjoy it.

There is something in the way he sings ordinary things. An interview. A date. A childhood dream. Some small fantasy he carried with him.

He turns them into songs.

I have been listening to him a lot now. His music feels soft enough to keep playing for hours. It does not matter what mood I am in. I still find my way back to it.

Today, I was listening to this song again. It stayed with me after it ended. Not because it says something new. But because it says something I needed.

Keep going.

Take your time.

Cross the finish line anyway.

Winners never quit.
