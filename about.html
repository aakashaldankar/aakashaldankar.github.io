I'm Aakash

I love to code things.

Not just write code. Build things. The kind of things that actually make a difference to someone, somewhere.

I read a lot. Business books. Biographies. Finance. There's something about understanding how people think, how they build, how they fail and try again. That stuff sticks with me longer than most things do.

Exploring new ideas. Sharing what I learn. Building what I imagine. That's the loop I live in.

I'm an introvert. But the quiet kind, not the lonely kind. Give me my people, the ones I actually care about and I'll show up. Outings. Long conversations. Movies and Anime that make you think.

Death Note. Code Geass. If you know, you know.

I've started writing lately. About the things I'm learning. The things I'm building. Putting thoughts into words makes them real in a way that thinking alone doesn't.

I work out too. Not to become a muscle man. Just to be fit. Strong. The kind of strong that lets you carry what life throws at you.

That's me. Still figuring it out. Still building.
